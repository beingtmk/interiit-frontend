Social Campaign

Mission

Sports has always been a part of our DNA. The spirit and exhilaration that competition brings, is something that cannot and should not be kept from anyone. India also has a lot of sporting potential that goes unnoticed and unidentified, often due to socio-economic realities like poverty, which needs redressal.

The Social campaign will see many events like Inter NGO Sports meet, Team outings, Tech for sports, and Impact league in collaboration with Imapactrun app etc.

With these twin objectives in mind, Inter IIT Sports Meet 2017 presents Kridayog.

Impact League

Impactrun is a pan IIT, month long competition, in collaboration with the app Impactrun. The app helps you can raise money for charity by walking and jogging on it, all while contributing kilometers to make your Alma mater win.

For every kilometer covered 10 rupees would be donated to whichever cause you choose to run for. All IITs can encourage students, alumni and/or residents, professors etc, to take part in the month long run. This campaign/competition is extremely flexible as anyone can log into the application and run, walk or jog at any time during the 31 day period.
