30TH September
The poster boy for Indian swimming, Rehan has competed at the most prestigious championships including the Beijing Olympics, Commonwealth Games, Asian Games and the Asian Indoor Games. He is a Gold medallist at the Malaysian, Singapore and Hong Kong Open, a Gold medallist at the Asian Indoor Games and a Silver medallist at the Asian Swimming Championships. Rehan holds the distinction of being India's Junior National Champion twice and Senior National Champion thrice. He was the Best Overall Athlete at the 2002 and 2007 National Games. Rehan currently holds 3 national records. In 2010, Rehan received the very prestigious Arjuna award in recognition of his outstanding contribution to sport from Pratibha Patil.

Atul Khatri is one of the India's most successful Stand-up comedians and YouTube personality. He is part of the East India Comedy, founded by Sorabh Pant which is the India's busiest comedy company with a record of 130 shows across the country in calendar year 2013. CNN-IBN recently rated him as one of the top 20 Comedians in India to watch out for He recently won 'CEOS GOT TALENT' trophy competing against 11 other CEOs. Atul Khatri also performs at The Comedy Store, The Canvas Laugh Factory in India and Abroad. He also performed in San Francisco for a fundraiser organized by American India Foundation.
